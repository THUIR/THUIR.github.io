About me

I am now working as professor of the Department of Computer Science and Technology, director of the Office for Research, as well as director of the Institute for Internet Judiciary in Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. I am also a visiting research professor of National University of Singapore and a visiting professor of National Institute of Informatics (NII) of Japan. My major research interests are in Web Search, User Behavior Analysis, and Natural Language Processing.

Recent Professional Activities

  • Our paper "Explicit factor models for explainable recommendation based on phrase-level sentiment analysis" won the Test of Time Award at SIGIR 2024.
  • Our paper "Scaling Laws For Dense Retrieval" won the Best Paper Award at SIGIR 2024.
  • I serve as Steering Committee co-Chair for SIGIR-AP.
  • I served as co-chair for SIGIR 2024 Short Paper Track and PC for Perspectives/DC/Tutorial papers.
  • I was recognized as a member of the ACM SIGIR Academy for "significant, cumulative contributions to the development of the field of information retrieval".
  • I serve as General Co-chair of NTCIR-18.
  • I am on the editorial board of JASIST and Information Retrieval Journal.
  • From 2017 to 2024, I serve as co-Editor-in-Chief of Foundations and Trends@ in Information Retrieval.

Datasets

Many of released datasets are located at the website of Tiangong AI Research Center (in Chinese). For example, we have a web corpus and a image dataset. Here are some datasets which are used in our recent studies:

  • TianGong-CRL Dataset. in our ASIST'20 Poster Investigating COVID-19-Related Query Logs of Chinese Search Engine Users.
  • Image Annotation Dataset. in our TOIS (2019) paper On Annotation Methodologies for Image Search Evaluation
  • User Behavior Dataset. in our WSDM'19 paper The influence of image search intents on user behavior and satisfaction.
  • Reading Attention Dataset. in our CIKM'18 paper Understanding Reading Attention Distribution during Relevance Judgement.
  • SRR Dataset. in our CIKM'18 paper Relevance Estimation with Multiple Information Sources on Search Engine Result Pages
  • Sogou-QCL Dataset. in our SIGIR'18 paper Sogou-QCL: A New Dataset with Click Relevance Label
  • SearchSuccess Dataset. in our WWW'18 paper "Satisfaction with Failure" or "Unsatisfied Success": Investigating the Relationship between Search Success and User Satisfaction.
